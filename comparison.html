BookArc Stand: $40.00

The first thing you should know about this stand it that it fits the iPad quite snugly. If you have your iPad in a case, it may not be able to fit into this stand. The Griffin A-frame iPad stand is a strange bird among iPad stands. First off, it's ugly. Your guests will never be able to guess what this thing is if left on the kitchen table. Secondly, the Griffin A-frame iPad stand is unnecessarily clunky. Why is the part of the thing that the iPad sits on so thick? It will cost you $50 to find out. The Griffin A-frame gets a C in our book.
